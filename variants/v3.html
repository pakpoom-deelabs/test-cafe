Our Story

Started from a
single obsession.

Brew & Co was never supposed to be a business. It began as an after-hours experiment — founder Mira Chen and two friends, a rented roaster, and an unhealthy amount of curiosity about what great coffee could taste like.

That obsession turned into a small pop-up, then a permanent space in Thong Lo, and eventually into a community of regulars who became the heartbeat of the place. We didn't build an audience. We found our people.

2018

Founded in a shared kitchen. First roast: a natural Ethiopian that changed everything.

2020

Opened the flagship café on Sukhumvit Soi 36. 28 seats. Always full by 9 AM.

2023

Launched direct-trade partnerships across three continents. Quality, not convenience.

Now

Still the same obsession. Just more people sharing it with us.
