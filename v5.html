Our Story

Built on Passion,
Brewed with Purpose.

Brew & Co was founded on the belief that specialty coffee is a language — one that connects farmers in Ethiopia, Honduras, and Colombia with the professionals of Bangkok who need more from their morning than caffeine.

Every bean is hand-selected, every roast profiled by our in-house team, every cup pulled with the precision you'd expect from a craft worth caring about.

The Craft

From origin to
your hands.

01

Direct Sourcing

We work directly with small-lot farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Honduras — traceability isn't a buzzword here, it's how we know what's in your cup.

02

Precision Roasting

Our roast profiles are developed over weeks of cupping. We roast in small batches twice a week to guarantee peak freshness — never more than 10 days from roast to cup.

03

Calibrated Daily

Every grinder is dialed in each morning. Extraction parameters shift with temperature and humidity. Consistency isn't accidental — it's engineered.

04

Served with Intention

Our team trains for months before serving a single cup. Every drink leaves the bar at the right temperature, in the right vessel, at the right moment.
